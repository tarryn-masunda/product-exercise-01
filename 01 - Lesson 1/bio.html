Who is Kim Nmajoon?

Kim Namjoon, known as RM, is a South Korean rapper, songwriter, and producer. Born on September 12, 1994, in Ilsan, South Korea, he is best known as the leader of BTS, one of the world’s most influential and best-selling music groups. With a passion for literature and poetry from a young age, RM’s deep, introspective lyrics reflect themes of identity, self-acceptance, and societal issues, resonating with fans globally. He’s not only fluent in English, largely self-taught, but also serves as BTS's spokesperson in many international settings. Beyond music, RM is recognized for his love of art and his efforts to promote youth empowerment and mental health awareness.
